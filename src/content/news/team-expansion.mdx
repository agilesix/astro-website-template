We're excited to announce the expansion of our team with new hires across design, development, and user research disciplines. This growth will allow us to better serve our government partners and take on more projects that improve citizen services.

## New Team Members

We've added 12 new team members across three key areas:

### Design Team
- 4 new UX designers specializing in accessibility
- 2 service designers with government experience

### Development Team
- 3 full-stack developers
- 2 front-end specialists focused on accessibility

### User Research Team
- 1 senior user researcher
- 2 research coordinators

## Why We're Growing

The demand for user-centered, accessible government services continues to grow. Our expansion allows us to:

- Take on more projects simultaneously
- Provide deeper expertise in specialized areas
- Reduce project timelines
- Maintain our high quality standards

## Our Commitment

As we grow, we remain committed to our core values:
- User-centered design
- Accessibility and inclusion
- Transparency and accountability
- Public service mission

We're excited to welcome our new team members and continue our work improving government services for all citizens.
