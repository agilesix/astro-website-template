We're honored to announce that our work on the Federal Benefits Portal has been recognized with the Excellence in Civic Technology Award from the Digital Government Institute.

## About the Award

The Excellence in Civic Technology Award recognizes organizations that demonstrate exceptional commitment to improving government services through innovative technology solutions. This year's award specifically highlights our work in:

- User-centered design
- Accessibility compliance
- Service delivery improvements

## The Project

The Federal Benefits Portal project involved a complete redesign of how citizens access and manage their benefits. Key achievements include:

- 40% reduction in application processing time
- 95% user satisfaction score
- Full Section 508 compliance
- Support for multiple languages

## Team Recognition

This award is a testament to the hard work of our entire team and our partners at the federal agency. We're grateful for the recognition and remain committed to our mission of improving government services for all citizens.

## What's Next

This recognition inspires us to continue pushing the boundaries of what's possible in civic technology. We're excited about upcoming projects and the opportunity to make an even greater impact.
